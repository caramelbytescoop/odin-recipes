Guacamole

Description

A simple and delicious guacamole recipe that’s perfect as a snack or appetizer. Creamy avocado, tangy lime, and the perfect touch of heat from jalapeño. Pair it with chips or use it as a topping for tacos!

Ingredients

  • 3 ripe avocados
  • 1 lime, juiced
  • 1/4 cup chopped onion
  • 1 small jalapeño, finely chopped (optional)
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon pepper
  • 1/2 cup chopped cilantro
  • 1 small tomato, diced (optional)

Steps

  1. Cut the avocados in half, remove the pits, and scoop the flesh into a large bowl.
  2. Use a fork to mash the avocados to your desired consistency.
  3. Add lime juice, onion, jalapeño (if using), garlic, salt, and pepper to the bowl and stir to combine.
  4. Fold in the chopped cilantro and tomato (if using).
  5. Taste and adjust seasoning if necessary (more salt, lime, or pepper). Serve immediately with tortilla chips, or cover with plastic wrap and refrigerate until ready to serve.
